02

We're on the phone within 5 minutes

Speed to lead is everything. A lead that gets a call in 5 minutes is worth far more than one chased the next day.

Our team contacts every single inquiry fast, while they are still hot, so none of them go cold or get scooped by the bloke down the road.

And we don't stop after one try. If someone doesn't pick up, we keep following up across calls, texts and email until we get a response, so no lead ever gets left to go to waste.

03

We qualify every lead

Not every inquiry is worth your time. We put every lead through a strict qualification sequence: project scope, price qualified, timeframe, and service area.

We only book quotes that we think are genuinely winnable, to save you wasting time on the road.

No time wasters
04

We book qualified leads straight into your calendar

Once a lead is qualified, we book them directly into your calendar for a site inspection or quote. You just turn up to real opportunities.

You get new quotes to do every week without lifting a finger on the admin.

Calendar filled weekly
05

We warm them up before you meet them

A booked lead is not a sold lead. The gap between booking and inspection is where most homeowners go cold, start second guessing, or ring around for other quotes. So we fill that gap.

We build pre-inspection nurture pages and email sequences that explain exactly why you are the right choice for their project. By the time you turn up, they already trust you and they are leaning towards yes.

Every step of the process is intentional and clear to the homeowner. No confusion about what happens next, no room for misunderstanding or frustration. Just a smooth path from inquiry to signed job.

Warm before you arrive

Winning quotes comes down to follow up and process, and that is the part most trades let slip. So we hand you the full back end to support it: detailed follow up and sales playbooks built for trade businesses, plus an open opportunities dashboard so you can see every live quote in one place.
